The Story of Nicole

Biography:

I was born in 1990 , the oldest of Four girls, a wife for 6 years, a mum to a fearless, kind and engergetic 4 year old boy.

I was born in Newmarket, Ontario, Canada. I moved to barrie in grade 3 when my parents split and stayed in barrie till half way through highschool (YES my mother made me move in highschool). I moved back to Newmarket when my mom met my step dad. We went from a house hold of 3 to 6 and my poor step dad was now outnumbered by us girls 5 to 1 ( mood swings and all).

I graduated highschool in 2008. Went to Seneca Collage and was a social worker/CYW for 10 hectic yet rewarding years. Then covid hit and like many people reevaluate my career and burn out it was causing with my home life. That leads me to where I am now Georgian Collage !

My BIGGEST achivement to date is becoming a mom. Before my son I would have said traveling the world or my sporting achivements in my youth. There is something about becoming a parent at least for myself changed my perspective on life.

My goal is to find a career I am passionate about again and contiune to keep pushing myself to be the BEST mother, wife , sister and daughter I can be.
